ABOUT MASKAD COMPANY LIMITED

Is a Tanzanian and locally owned company registered by Business registration and Licensing Agency and Constructors Registration Board (CRB) as a Company Dealing with Civil Engineering and Information/Communication Technology with registration numbers C6/2556/11/2022 (CIVIL WORKS CONTRACTOR), SPC2/260/11/2022 (SPECIALIST CIVIL WORKS CONTRACTOR) and B6/1626/11/2022 (BUILDING WORKS CONTRACTOR).


Maskad Company was established in January 2022, with four key participants for the General Purpose of offering Engineering Construction and Construction Project management in Geita Region and Tanzania in general. Therefore Maskad Company Limited is dedicated to organizations or Individuals seeking construction services and Information and Communication Technology services.
